In 2005 the film adaptation of The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy was released. Douglas Adams had helped write the script before his untimely passing and so the film was dedicated to his memory. It was received mostly well, grossing over $100 million at the box office, and starring well-known actors such as Martin Freeman, Zooey Deschanel, Stephen Fry, and Alan Rickman to name a few.
